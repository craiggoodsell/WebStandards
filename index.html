Your Universe

Your apart of something Huge! Imagine you are on the equator and you are standing still. You are still moving over 1000 mph while Earth rotates. On top of that, you are moving over 60k mph around the sun.

Then the solar system is moving through the galaxy at over 400k mph. For the sake of ending the math, we will stop at the galaxy moving through the universe at over 2 million mph. When you add everything up you are moving at well over 2.4 million mph while you are standing still.

Galaxery is here to provide you with some of the scenery that you pass without being able to really see what it is that you are looking at. Our gallaries cover Stars, Planets, Moons, and other celestial objects. go ahead and click on one of our navigation links to start traveling the heavens
